About SnowySec

Hello, I am SnowySec, an information security researcher, student, and engineer. By day I work at a large Fortune 20 bank in their cybersecurity department, scanning and remediating application security vulnerabilties. By night I attend a very large university where I am enrolled in their NSA/ABET accredited cybersecurity program. I spend a lot of time learning more and more about cybersecurity topics and playing around in my lab. Currently my biggest interests are reverse engineering as well as web application hacking.

For fun I generally like to play video games, especially any single-player or couch co-op games from Nintendo, or massive-world RPGs such as Fallout. I have a gorgeous wife, a silly Labrador, and two mischevious little kitties.

On this blog you will find various write-ups of HackTheBox owns and security tutorials. I hope you enjoy your time here!
